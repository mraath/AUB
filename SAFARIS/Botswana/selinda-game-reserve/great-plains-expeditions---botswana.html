Great Plains Expeditions Botswana

Experience

Have you ever dreamed of an authentic, off-the-beaten-track wilderness experience?The Great Plains Expeditions offers a 4-night (set departure) Botswana cross country safari through the private Selinda Reserve in either an easterly or westerly direction.Adventurous safari-goers will witness Selinda’s best wildlife sightings, including leopard, pangolin & wild dog.On this cross country safari, exhilarating activities will take guests through an area that is home to the highest density of elephants globally & in the spirit of genuine expedition, guests can even get involved in collecting data that will assist with anti-poaching.Here, in one of Botswana’s most remote regions, guests can also encounter some of Africa’s most incredible elephant & buffalo interactions.

Stay

On each 4-night departure, the Great Plains Expeditions offers 3 beautiful camps set within the heart of the Selinda Reserve. One night is spent at the first & last camp, & 2 nights are spent at the second camp where excellent walking & canoeing can be enjoyed.Small & intimate, a Great Plains Expeditions safari can accommodate a maximum of 8 guests sharing 4 tents, each with 2 single beds. Children of 12 years & older are welcome to join this once-in-a-lifetime safari experience through Botswana.

Activities

A range of exciting wildlife viewing activities is included in your stay.Open 4x4 game drives will take you straight into the action while guided walking safaris & canoeing along the Selinda Spillway (water levels permitting) offer a different perspective.Frequent sightings of cheetah can be expected as well as some of the best, & possibly even final, large concentrations of sable & roan antelope.

Have you ever dreamed of an authentic, off-the-beaten-track wilderness experience? The Great Plains Expeditions offers a 4-night (set departure) Botswana cross country safari through the private Selinda Reserve in either an easterly or westerly direction.

Selinda Camp

Selinda Camp overlooks the Selinda Spillway, a unique seasonal waterway that links the Okavango Delta with the Linyanti Swamps. Packed with some of Africa’s wildest animals, the region enjoys an abundance of game, including predators, antelope &amp; over 300 bird species.

Selinda Explorers Camp

Selinda Explorers Camp overlooks the Selinda Spillway, a unique seasonal waterway that links the Okavango Delta with the Linyanti Swamps. Its sister camp, Selinda, is located about one hour west. Packed with some of Africa’s wildest animals, the region enjoys an abundance of game, including predators, antelope &amp; over 300 bird species.

Zarafa Camp

Zarafa Camp overlooks the magnificent Zibadianja Lagoon – the source of the world-famous Savute Channel. The Selinda Reserve is famous for its massive herds of elephants &amp; large populations of predators, such as lion, leopard, cheetah &amp; wild dog. Abundant plains game can also be found here, including kudu, buffalo, zebra &amp; giraffe.
