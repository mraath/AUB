Nyamatusi Camp

Nyamatusi Camp

Experience

Nestled between a grove of large mahogany & sausage trees lies Nyamatusi Camp – one of Mana Pool National Park’s most luxurious safari camps.With air-conditioning & private plunge pools in every suite, guests can experience this wildlife-rich area in style, while still adhering to an eco-friendly & sustainable design.Mana Pools’ complex ecosystem supports a wealth of game, including cheetah, wild dog, lion & leopard, which makes this area attractive to safari enthusiasts.Because the Zambezi River’s banks swell during the summer rains, Nyamatusi is a seasonal camp that only operates between April & November – the best time to go on safari!

Stay

Boasting 6 spacious tented suites, Nyamatusi Camp is small & intimate – offering a remote safari experience in a pristine wilderness setting.Each of the suites has uninterrupted views of the Zambezi River & includes an en-suite bathroom with indoor & outdoor shower, air-conditioning, a pilates mat & weights as well as a private deck with a plunge pool where lazy days are spent.The double-story main area features a library, dining room, bar & viewing deck with splash pool.

Activities

Nyamatusi Camp has a variety of exciting safari activities on the menu. Some of these include open 4x4 game drives, walking safaris & catch-&-release fishing.The Lower Zambezi River is the ideal setting for an adventurous canoe safari. Guests can paddle along the tranquil waters & look for wildlife such as hippos, crocodiles, elephants & a variety of birds.

Lodges
Lodges
Chikwenya Camp

The seasonal Chikweyna Camp (open April to November) enjoys an idyllic location in an exclusive-use area in one of Zimbabwe’s prime wildlife regions: Mana Pools National Park. With jaw-dropping views of the Zambezi River, its floodplains &amp; the distant mountains of the Rift Valley escarpment, photographers often feel like they are in ‘wildlife paradise.’

John's Camp

John’s Camp lies under a canopy of giant trees with views stretching over the Zambezi River floodplains in Mana Pools National Park – a national park &amp; World Heritage Site. Mana Pools is big game country at its best &amp; has long enjoyed a reputation as one of the country’s best off-the-beaten-track safari destinations.

Kanga Camp

The seasonal Kanga Camp (only open from May – October) is set in a crowd-free private concession within one of Zimbabwe’s prime wildlife regions: Mana Pools National Park. Kanga Camp offers guests an authentic, adventurous safari experience with a touch of luxury.

Little Ruckomechi

Little Ruckomechi is located within the private Ruckomechi Concession &amp; one of only a handful of lodges scattered around Zimbabwe’s Mana Pools National Park. The camp offers a true wilderness experience combined with ‘just the right amount’ of luxury. Adventure seekers who are looking for an off-the-beaten-track safari experience will adore Little Ruckomechi.

Nyamatusi Camp

Nestled between a grove of large mahogany &amp; sausage trees lies Nyamatusi Camp – one of Mana Pool National Park’s most luxurious safari camps. With air-conditioning &amp; private plunge pools in every suite, guests can experience this wildlife-rich area in style, while still adhering to an eco-friendly &amp; sustainable design.

Ruckomechi Camp

Ruckomechi Camp is located on the banks of the hippo-filled Zambezi River &amp; one of only a handful of lodges scattered around Zimbabwe’s Mana Pools National Park. The camp offers a true wilderness experience combined with ‘just the right amount’ of luxury. Adventure seekers who are looking for an off-the-beaten-track safari experience will adore Ruckomechi Camp.

Sapi Explorers Camp

Sapi Explorers Camp is nestled within a privately-run wildlife reserve – the 120 000 hectares Sapi Concession, next to Zimbabwe’s legendary Mana Pools National Park. The camp lies quite literally a stone’s throw from the Zambezi River, where plenty of animal activity takes place.

Zambezi Expeditions

The semi-permanent Zambezi Expeditions is a tented camp (only open from May to October) in the heart of Mana Pools National Park. Small &amp; intimate, Zambezi Expeditions is the ultimate safari destination for adventure-seeking travellers looking for an authentic, back-to-basics safari experience with a little bit of luxury.
