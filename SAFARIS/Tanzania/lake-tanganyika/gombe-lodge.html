Gombe Lodge

Gombe Lodge

Experience

Set on the northern shore of Lake Tanganyika in Tanzania’s Gombe Stream National Park – famed for its dense chimpanzee population - lies Gombe Lodge – an exclusive & authentic tented camp that offers a unique wildlife experience.Since the camp enjoys an ideal lakeshore setting, guests will not only be able to track active chimp families through this ancient emerald forest but will also be able to enjoy water activities like snorkelling, fishing & kayaking.Lake Tanganyika is the second largest freshwater lake world & attracts a variety of wildlife, like olive baboons, energetic vervet, red-tailed & red colobus monkeys, bush pigs, hippos & leopards as well over 200 bird species.

Stay

Accommodation at Gombe Lodge consists of 7 spacious walk-in forest tents built on elevated timber platforms under the shade of giant mango trees.With African-inspired décor, each tent features an en-suite bathroom & a private deck overlooking the lush forest.The combined lounge & dining area at the main lodge is built above the forest floor offering spectacular views over the lake & mountains. There is also a lodge library for keen readers.

Activities

Gombe Lodge is all about the African jungle & has a wide range of exciting land & water activities on offer.These include chimpanzee trekking safaris as well as water sports like kayaking, boating, swimming or fishing. Guided forest walks & bird watching are also popular.Guests can also visit a neighbouring fishing village to learn more about the local culture.

Gombe Lodge

Set on the northern shore of Lake Tanganyika in Tanzania’s Gombe Stream National Park – famed for its dense chimpanzee population - lies Gombe Lodge – an exclusive &amp; authentic tented camp that offers a unique wildlife experience. Since the camp enjoys an ideal lakeshore setting, guests will not only be able to track active chimp families through this ancient emerald forest but will also be able to enjoy water activities like snorkelling, fishing &amp; kayaking.

Greystoke Mahale

Set on a remote beach on the shores of Lake Tanganyika in the chimp-inhabited forests of the Mahale Mountains in West Tanzania, lies Greystoke Mahale – an intimate &amp; exclusive jungle retreat. The Mahale Mountains National Park hosts the largest known population of chimpanzees in the world as well as a variety of other wildlife, like leopard, bushbuck, bush pig &amp; many bird species.

Lupita Island

Set on a secluded island on the beautiful Lake Tanganyika, Lupita Island is a luxurious haven for romantics who wish to soak up the pristine Tanzanian environment. A honeymooner’s paradise, the 130-acre Lupita Island accommodates only 28 guests, ensuring an intimate &amp; private stay.

Mahale Lodge

Tucked away in one of the most remote corners of Tanzania along the shores of Lake Tanganyika, lies Mahale Lodge – a luxurious yet off-the-beaten-track island retreat. The thick rainforest of the Mahale Mountains is home to the densest known population of wild, endangered chimpanzees &amp; therefore offers a unique opportunity to trek them on foot.
