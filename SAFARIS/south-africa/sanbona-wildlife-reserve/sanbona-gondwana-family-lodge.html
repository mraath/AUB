Sanbona Gondwana Family Lodge

Sanbona Gondwana Family Lodge

Experience

Sanbona Wildlife Reserve boasts 3 luxury lodges & 1 adventure camp: Sanbona Dwyka Tented Lodge, Sanbona Tilney Manor, Sanbona Gondwana Family Lodge & Sanbona Explorer Camp.Sanbona Gondwana Family Lodge, as the name suggests, is a haven for families (or friends) & overlooks the spectacular Anysberg Mountains & Bellair Dam.Not only is the lodge located in a malaria-free Big 5 wildlife reserve, but it also boasts interleading rooms, a child-friendly pool as well as indoor & outdoor play areas. Kids are a big deal at Gondwana & their dedicated Kids Programme includes children of all ages.The cherry on top is that the lodge is situated only a 3-hour drive from Cape Town, making this an ideal safari choice for guests visiting the Western Cape.

Stay

There are several room options at Gondwana Family Lodge. Six suites are located on the ground floor & have private decks with beautiful views over the bush. There are also 6 upstairs suites with private balconies looking out over the bushveld.Each of the 12 suites can accommodate a maximum of 2 adults & 1 child. (The child will be accommodated on a sleeper couch.) Four pairs of suites are interleading, which makes them ideal for families traveling with young children.Each suite has an en-suite bathroom with a shower, a free-standing bath & twin basins.Communal areas include a comfortable lounge with fireplace, an indoor & outdoor dining area, an open-air boma, an outdoor pool with a shallow area & a children’s playroom. There is also a gift boutique & a Relaxation Retreat with a steam room.

Activities

A range of exciting activities is available, including open 4x4 game drives, boat safaris on the Bellair Dam (subject to water levels), wilderness walks, stargazing, bird watching & visits to San rock art sites.Sanbona Gondwana’s children’s programme will keep the little ones entertained from beginning to end! Guests can also enjoy pampering time at the Relaxation Retreat.
