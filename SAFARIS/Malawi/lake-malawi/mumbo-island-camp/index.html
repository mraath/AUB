Mumbo Island Camp

Experience

Mumbo Island Camp is a rustic, yet comfortable camp situated on a pristine island in Lake Malawi.The camp lies within an exclusive concession & forms part of Lake Malawi National Park – a World Heritage Site - & the first freshwater marine reserve in the world.The park is home to more than 500 species of fish, of which over 90 per cent are endemic. This little piece of paradise offers some of the best fres water scuba diving in the world & the best snorkelling on the lake.

Stay

Mumbo Island Camp is small & intimate & adheres to the principles of eco-architecture by blending into the natural landscape & large granite boulders.There are 5 tents & 1 family tent with 4 single beds – all constructed of timber, thatch & reed. The tents are set on raised wooden decks with separate eco-toilets & bucket showers to help preserve the environment. Each tent also features a hammock where guests can relax & take in the idyllic lake view.The main area comprises a dining area, bar & water sports gazebo of timber & thatch.

Activities

Mumbo Island Camp has plenty of activities to offer. For the more energetic, there are kayaking, snorkelling & scuba diving. Others may enjoy swimming, walking or bird watching.There is also the option of boating or paddling to explore the granitic islands of Lake Malawi.

Kaya Mawa

Kaya Mawa enjoys a spectacular location on the crescent-shaped bay on Likoma Island in Lake Malawi. This remote island is surrounded by crystal-clear water, mango trees &amp; ancient baobabs. Kaya Mawa is the ideal choice for honeymooners or couples looking for total relaxation. Warm &amp; friendly service is one of the things that stand out at Kaya Mawa – one of the key elements of having a wonderful holiday.

Nkwichi Lodge

Nkwichi Lodge boasts a beautiful location on a white, sandy beach in the Mozambican section of Lake Malawi (known in Mozambique as Lake Niassa). Utterly remote, the lodge has been built to blend in with the surrounding landscape &amp; offers expansive views over the lake.

Pumulani Lodge

Pumulani Lodge is set on the lush hills above beautiful Lake Malawi &amp; is the perfect holiday destination for adventure seekers or those looking for a relaxing, tranquil getaway. The lodge boasts a private, white sandy beach lapped by clear blue waters. Guests can relax on the sandy beach or enjoy an elevated view of the lake from the stylish infinity pool.

The Makokola Retreat

The Makokola Retreat is a charming resort-style beach hotel set among beautiful gardens on the southern coast of Lake Malawi. The resort features spacious lawns, palm trees &amp; an endless beach – making this the ideal spot to relax after an African safari.
