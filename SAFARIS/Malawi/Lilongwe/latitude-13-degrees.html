Latitude 13 Degrees

Latitude 13 Degrees

Experience

Latitude 13 Degrees is a stylish & contemporary boutique hotel located in Lilongwe’s quiet & leafy district. Standing out above the rest, the hotel raises the bar for urban accommodation in Malawi’s capital city.Latitude 13 Degrees is only a 10-minute drive from the CBD & a 30-minute drive from Kamuzu Airport, offering guests a sophisticated level of comfort before or after a journey through Malawi.

Stay

There are 9 spacious suites set in a tropical garden, each complete with a lounge & private terrace. The en-suite bathrooms boast large sunken baths & walk-in rain showers.The central area features a restaurant, swimming pool, bar, gym & wellness spa where relaxing treatments can be enjoyed.

Activities

A chauffeur-driven car is available for hire, so guests can explore the city in comfort. Their on-site restaurant, Latitude, is widely regarded as the best in Lilongwe.The attentive concierge will assist guests in planning their daily activities.
