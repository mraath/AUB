Why Lake Kivu Serena Hotel?

Why Lake Kivu Serena Hotel?

Experience

Lake Kivu Serena Hotel boasts a spectacular location on a private stretch of beach on the shores of Lake Kivu. This is a place of astonishing beauty where pristine beaches & sparkling waters come together in a breathtaking fashion.Lake Kivu is Africa’s 6th largest lake, so guests can enjoy a variety of exciting land & water-based recreational activities.Lake Kivu Serena Hotel is an hour’s drive from Volcanoes National Park, making this a destination to unwind after a gorilla-trekking safari.

Stay

The hotel offers 66 rooms in total, divided between superior rooms, family rooms & executive suites. All rooms have lavish en-suite bathrooms. The family rooms are the same size as the superior rooms, but instead, have 2 queen beds instead of 1 king bed.The executive suites are almost double the size as the superior & family rooms, & feature bathrooms with both tub & shower, an integrated workspace, a private sitting room with a guest washroom & a furnished veranda overlooking Lake Kivu.The main area includes the main restaurant, a cocktail bar & a beach café. Outdoor facilities include a swimming pool, deck, garden as well as tennis & volleyball courts.

Activities

Lake Kivu Serena Hotel features an activities club, allowing guests convenient access to boating & canoeing on the lake. Guests can also visit Idjiwi Island in the middle of the lake, visit hot springs, crystal-clear swimming areas, or try traditional night fishing. Hiking is also on offer for the adventurous at heart.Other facilities include a gift shop, health spa & gym.
