Why Shamwari Sindile?

Why Shamwari Sindile?

Experience

Shamwari Game Reserve is one of the most established wildlife areas in the Eastern Cape & is renowned for its Big 5 wildlife viewing, luxurious lodges & exceptional conservation initiatives.Shamwari has 7 different lodges & 1 explorer camp, each providing distinct experiences: Sindile, Riverdene, Lobengula, Eagles Crag, Bayethe, Sarili, Long Lee Manor & Explorer Camp - each with its own style & brand of luxury.

Stay

Shamwari Sindile is a luxurious & intimate safari lodge, catering to individuals over the age of 16, ensuring a relaxing safari experience.The lodge has 9 premier tents, each one elevated above the surrounding bush, allowing uninhibited views. Each tent is equipped with an en-suite bathroom, air-conditioning, bath & shower, outdoor shower, indoor fireplace as well as a lounge & dining area. The highlight of these tents is that they each also boasts a private heated plunge pool where days can be spent relaxing or reading.The main lodge provides excellent vantages over the Bushman’s River & surrounding plains. Here you will find a lounge area, dining room with fireplace, bar & lounge with fireplace, deck & firepit, boma & Relaxation Retreat.

Activities

Shamwari’s diverse wilderness area makes Big 5 game viewing easy. The reserve is home to lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo & rhino as well as plenty of plains game & over 200 varieties of raptors & birds.Private daily safari drives are conducted in open 4x4 vehicles. Your dedicated ranger will take you on a journey through the bushveld & ensure close encounters with South Africa’s wildlife. Nature walks, spa treatments & photographic courses can also be arranged.Guests staying at Shwamwari can also get involved in the Wildlife Rehabilitation Programme or the Born Free Big Cat Sanctuary where the focus is on conservation.
