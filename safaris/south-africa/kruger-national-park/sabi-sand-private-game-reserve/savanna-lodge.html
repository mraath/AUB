Why Savanna Lodge?

Why Savanna Lodge?

Experience

Savanna Lodge is situated in the world-renowned Sabi Sand Reserve within the Greater Kruger National Park conservancy.Savanna lies in an area where Big 5 sightings are so frequent, it is not unusual to see lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo & rhino in a single game drive.

Stay

Accommodation at Savanna consists of 4 luxury suites, 3 executive suites & 1 Savanna Suite.Each luxury suite has an en-suite bathroom with a bath, indoor & outdoor shower. The bedroom opens onto a private patio with a deck & private plunge pool. Sweeping views of the waterhole can be enjoyed from the comfort of your viewing deck.Each executive suites are larger & includes a sitting area.The main area at Savanna Lodge features a large dining room & open-air boma where dinners are enjoyed under the stars. Savanna is famous for its warm African hospitality, so be sure to arrive as a guest & leave as a friend.The Savanna Suite provides the ultimate home-away-from-home safari experience. There are 2 bedrooms on either side of an inter-connecting lounge. Each bedroom has an en-suite bathroom that opens onto a patio with a plunge pool & wooden deck. Beautiful waterhole views can be enjoyed from here. The communal area at the Savanna Suite features a lounge with comfortable sofas, a TV & DVD centre, a cosy reading nook, a kitchen & fireplace. Guests staying at the Savanna Suite will have a dedicated ranger, tracker, chef & butler.

Activities

Activities include open 4x4 game drives, nature walks, Shangaan village tours & spa treatments. The in-house beauty therapist can offer a range of treatments in the privacy of your room.
