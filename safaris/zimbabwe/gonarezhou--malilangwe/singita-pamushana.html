Why Singita Pamushana?

Why Singita Pamushana?

Experience

Exclusive Singita Pamushana Lodge is situated within the 130,000-hectare private Malilangwe Wildlife Reserve, sharing its southern boundary with Gonarezhou National Park.At Singita Pamushana the focus is on savouring the wildlife you encounter, rather than ticking off a checklist. Guests can therefore expect a memorable & luxurious safari experience where more than 36 mammals & 125 bird species can be seen.

Stay

Pamushana comprises 4 one-bedroom spacious suites, 1 two-bedroom family suite (ideal for 2 adults & 2 children), 1 three-bedroom family suite (ideal for 2 adults & 4 children) & 2 two-bedroom suites (ideal for 4 adults travelling together) built high on the cliff-side under a canopy of trees, offering spectacular views of Lake Malilangwe.Each suite offers the ultimate luxury in a truly African setting & features an en-suite bathroom with an indoor & outdoor shower, private plunge pool, double-sided fireplace & game viewing deck with Swarovski spotting scope.The central lodge area includes a wellness spa, gym, tennis courts, a 9-hole golf course, mountain-bike track & archery course.

Activities

Activities at Singita Pamushana are ample.Some of these include open 4x4 game viewing, stargazing safaris, swimming, fishing, baking with the chefs as well as tennis, golf, biking, archery & gym.Guests can also book relaxing treatments at the wellness spa. Babysitting facilities can also be arranged.
