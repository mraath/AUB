Why Little Ongava?

Why Little Ongava?

Experience

Little Ongava is one of Namibia’s most luxurious & exclusive safari camps. Built on a rocky outcrop in the Ongava Game Reserve to the south of Etosha, the lodge provides awe-inspiring views over the reserve.The Etosha National Park is one of Africa’s largest game parks & boasts a variety of wildlife & large herds of plains game. The elusive black rhino can also be found here.

Stay

Little Ongava, as the name suggests, consists of only 3 spacious, luxurious & private suites. Here guests will experience remote exclusivity like nowhere else in the country.Each suite features a private plunge pool, a sala & an en suite bathroom with both indoor & outdoor showers for those wishing to get closer to nature. The bath has a magnificent view over the surrounding reserve.The main lodge consists of a dining & lounge area, both looking out over the waterhole below.

Activities

Excellent photographic opportunities can be enjoyed from the 4x4 game drive vehicle or animal hide overlooking the waterhole.Frequent sightings of lion, elephant, gemsbok, springbok, red hartebeest & white & black rhino can be expected. For the adventurous at heart - white rhino can also be tracked on foot.

Mushara Bush Camp

Ongava Lodge

Onguma Bush Camp

Plains game roam freely on the reserve &amp; predators are common residents in the area.
