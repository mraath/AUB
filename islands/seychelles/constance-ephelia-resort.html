Why Constance Ephelia Resort?

Why Constance Ephelia Resort?

Experience

The beautiful, family-friendly Constance Ephelia Resort is located on the northwest coast of Mahe island & is – without a doubt – one of the most popular resorts in the Seychelles.The resort overlooks the Port Launay National Marine Park, offering a treasure trove of discovery for divers & snorkelers.Constance Ephelia boasts 2 stunning white, sandy beaches lapped by the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean.Guests can enjoy a variety of fantastic on-site resort facilities or try their luck at complimentary water sports like snorkelling, kayaking, paddle boarding & windsurfing.

Stay

The resort offers 7 types of stylish & luxurious accommodation. These consist of 42 tropical garden view rooms, 184 junior suites, 40 senior suites, 22 family villas, 16 beach villas, 8 hillside villas & the opulent presidential villa.The tropical garden view rooms have private balconies with direct garden access. The junior suites enjoy stunning ocean views & a spacious indoor seating area with a sofa bed & the senior suites offer more space, along with a private sun terrace.Family villas are either two or three-bedroom units featuring private gardens & pools. The beach villas & hillside villas offer one or two-bedroom options, each with a private pool, with the difference that the beach villas overlook the beach & the hillside villas are perched on a hill.The crème de la crème of Constance Ephelia is the opulent presidential villa that comes complete with a private gym, spa & infinity pool.Resort facilities include 4 swimming pools, 2 spas, a dining area & Kids Club with zipline & climbing wall.

Activities

Constance Ephelia Resort offers a range of exciting water sports, such as diving, snorkelling, stand-up paddleboarding, windsurfing, kayaking, pedal boats, catamaran sailing & fishing.Guests can also enjoy land-based sports like tennis or mountain bike excursions.
