  2. Beach villas located just meters from the ocean

Denis Private Island Lodge

Denis Private Island Lodge

Experience

Denis Private Island, a 375-acre private coral island is located amid the warm waters of the Indian Ocean in the beautiful Seychelles.The island is home to lush palm trees & white powdery beaches, ensuring an authentic island experience. Many will agree that this is a tropical paradise that is all about simple exclusivity.Denis Island offers a relaxing beach atmosphere & exceptional service. Guests staying here will be in good hands as the staff will cater to your every whim.The PADI dive centre provides diving lessons & excursions to explore the tropical waters for turtles, dolphins & even whale sharks. The island has a great reputation for deep-sea fishing, so guests can charter a vessel & crew to find out what is lurking in the waters below.

Stay

The island is home to 25 free-standing cottages & villas, scattered at various walking distances from the beach & separated by the island’s natural vegetation.There are 3 beach cottages, 10 deluxe beach cottages, 10 beachfront spa cottages & a beach villa.The beach cottages lie directly on the beachfront & feature private sun loungers & a furnished sea-facing veranda. The deluxe beach cottages are sea-facing & feature an open terrace leading into a private garden & onto the beach. They come complete with sun loungers, a personal sala & daybed.Exquisite beachfront spa cottages lie directly on the beachfront. Each spa cottage boasts a secluded deck with a sala & day bed as well as a private courtyard & garden with a massage pavilion.The beach villa has a private beachfront setting, with an extensive outdoor living & relaxation area complete with a plunge pool & daybed. The private courtyard features twin alfresco showers.Resort facilities include a library, swimming pool & bar.

Activities

Apart from diving & snorkelling, guests can enjoy canoeing, village visits, tennis, jogging or bird watching.
