Why The Oyster Box?

Why The Oyster Box?

Experience

The stylish Oyster Box is perched on the edge of the Indian Ocean & offers discerning travellers an elegant retreat close to many of Durban’s top attractions.The hotel is a fusion of old-world charm & modern-day convenience, boasting 2 swimming pools (one with an ocean view & the other one in the gardens that is for over 12’s only), a spa with jacuzzi & hammam as well as a fully-equipped fitness centre.The cherry on top is that guests can enjoy direct access to the beach with essentials like umbrellas, beach bags, toys & even sun protection lotion.Afternoon High Teas are an affair to remember. An indulgent feast of cakes, pastries & sandwiches awaits each day between lunch & dinner. In the evenings, guests can retreat to the 24-seater cinema & watch a classic movie while eating fresh popcorn.

Stay

The Oyster Box us 98 well-appointed rooms, all with air-conditioning & en-suite bathrooms.Rooms include garden villa lofts & classic garden facing rooms looking out over the hotel’s lush & tranquil gardens while classic sea-facing rooms & luxury sea-facing rooms have balconies that overlook Umhlanga’s iconic lighthouse & beautiful coastline.Sea facing cabanas are situated on the lower ground level with individual terraces & offer stunning views of the water’s edge while sea-facing luxury suites boast separate lounge areas & glorious views of the Indian Ocean. In addition, sea-facing deluxe suites include private plunge pools.Sea facing family rooms consist of 2 bedrooms & each superior garden villa has a private plunge pool, terrace, lounge & bar. The presidential suite is split over 2 levels & offers only the best in beachfront luxury.Hotel facilities include a private cinema, 2 swimming pools, 3 restaurants, a gym, spa, library, bar & Kids Club.

Activities

Guests can visit the nearby uShaka Marine World, Gateway Theatre of Shopping & the Sun Coast casino as well as a variety of amazing swimming & surfing beaches.

Canelands Beach Club & Sp...

The laidback Canelands Beach Club &amp; Spa boasts a spectacular location overlooking the beach at Salt Rock on the subtropical Kwazulu-Natal coast. The charming town of Ballito is nearby &amp; King Saka International Airport is only a 15-minute drive away, making this an ideal base from which to explore the surrounding area or as an overnight stay en route to the Battlefields or Phinda Game Reserve.

Teremok Marine Lodge

Teremok Marine Lodge is situated in beautiful Umhlanga, one of the most popular beach destinations on Durban’s north coast. Located near a handful of excellent swimming beaches &amp; within driving distance of popular attractions like uShaka Marine World &amp; Gateway shopping complex, Teremok is an ideal base from which to explore the surrounding area.

The Oyster Box

The stylish Oyster Box is perched on the edge of the Indian Ocean &amp; offers discerning travellers an elegant retreat close to many of Durban’s top attractions. The hotel is a fusion of old-world charm &amp; modern-day convenience, boasting 2 swimming pools (one with an ocean view &amp; the other one in the gardens that is for over 12’s only), a spa with jacuzzi &amp; hammam as well as a fully-equipped fitness centre.

Zimbali Resort

Zimbali Resort is situated inside the elite Zimbali Coastal Estate on the beautiful Dolphin Coast in Kwazulu-Natal. The resort boasts sensational views of the Indian Ocean &amp; offers its guests a 5-star service with the greatest attention to detail.
