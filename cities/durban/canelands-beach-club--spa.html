Why Canelands Beach Club & Spa?

Why Canelands Beach Club & Spa?

Experience

The laidback Canelands Beach Club & Spa boasts a spectacular location overlooking the beach at Salt Rock on the subtropical Kwazulu-Natal coast.The charming town of Ballito is nearby & King Saka International Airport is only a 15-minute drive away, making this an ideal base from which to explore the surrounding area or as an overnight stay en route to the Battlefields or Phinda Game Reserve.The hotel boasts awe-inspiring ocean views from the deck & offers guests direct access to a gorgeous, sandy beach.Food & drinks can be served directly on the beach, so guests can enjoy relaxing lunches from the comfort of their sun loungers. This is luxury beachfront living at its best.

Stay

Canelands Beach Club & Spa features 10 luxury rooms, each equipped with air-conditioning & an en-suite bathroom. There are include 6 standard garden rooms, 2 superior rooms & 2 deluxe suites.The garden rooms are located downstairs, while the superior rooms & deluxe suites are located upstairs, both of which offer uninterrupted sea views.Hotel facilities include a large deck, a 25m infinity pool with sun loungers, a comfortable lounge & a wellness spa that offers a range of relaxing treatments.

Activities

Canelands guests will have direct access to a stretch of beach just steps from the main deck. There is also a swimming beach a short drive away. Guests should also keep an eye out for dolphins frolicking in the water – an amazing start to the day!On-site beach soccer & volleyball are always a favourite amongst younger guests.The upscale neighbourhood offers plenty of activities, including world-class golfing, horse-back riding trips, gourmet dining & water sports.Durban can be reached in under an hour’s drive.

Teremok Marine Lodge

Teremok Marine Lodge is situated in beautiful Umhlanga, one of the most popular beach destinations on Durban’s north coast. Located near a handful of excellent swimming beaches &amp; within driving distance of popular attractions like uShaka Marine World &amp; Gateway shopping complex, Teremok is an ideal base from which to explore the surrounding area.

The Oyster Box

The stylish Oyster Box is perched on the edge of the Indian Ocean &amp; offers discerning travellers an elegant retreat close to many of Durban’s top attractions. The hotel is a fusion of old-world charm &amp; modern-day convenience, boasting 2 swimming pools (one with an ocean view &amp; the other one in the gardens that is for over 12’s only), a spa with jacuzzi &amp; hammam as well as a fully-equipped fitness centre.

Zimbali Resort

Zimbali Resort is situated inside the elite Zimbali Coastal Estate on the beautiful Dolphin Coast in Kwazulu-Natal. The resort boasts sensational views of the Indian Ocean &amp; offers its guests a 5-star service with the greatest attention to detail.
