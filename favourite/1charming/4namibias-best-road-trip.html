4namibia's Best Road Trip

1Charming

What We Love
  1. Explore Namibia at your own pace (self-drive)
  2. Capture the perfect photograph
  3. Take a scenic hot air balloon safari over Sossusvlei’s red sand dunes
  4. Search for Namibia’s iconic desert-adapted elephants
  5. Enjoy magnificent stargazing in remote settings
  6. Experience phenomenal Big 5 wildlife viewing
  7. ## Overview
  8. Day 1:
  9. Upon arrival at Windhoek International Airport, pick up your rental car & self-drive to The Olive Exclusive in Windhoek, Namibia.
  10. Overnight at The Olive Exclusive in a junior suite on a bed & breakfast basis.
  11. Day 2:
  12. Self-drive to Kulala Desert Lodge in Sossusvlei, Namibia.
  13. Day 2 – 5:
  14. Spend 3 nights at Kulala Desert Lodge in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  15. During your stay, you will take a scenic hot air balloon flight over the red sand dunes, followed by a lovely champagne breakfast.
  16. Day 5:
  17. Self-drive to Swakopmond Guesthouse in Swakopmund, Namibia.
  18. Day 5 – 7:
  19. Spend 2 nights at Swakopmund Guesthouse in a luxury room on a bed & breakfast basis.
  20. Day 7:
  21. Self-drive to Damaraland Camp in Damaraland, Namibia.
  22. Day 7 – 10:
  23. Spend 3 nights at Damaraland Camp in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  24. Day 10:
  25. Self-drive to Andersson’s at Ongava in Etosha, Namibia.
  26. Day 10 – 13:
  27. Spend 3 nights at Andersson’s at Ongava in a luxury suite on a fully inclusive basis.
  28. Day 13:
  29. Self-drive to The Olive Exclusive in Windhoek, Namibia.
  30. Overnight at The Olive Exclusive in a junior suite on a bed & breakfast basis.
  31. Day 14:
  32. Self-drive to Windhoek International Airport & drop off your rental car.
  33. Depart Windhoek International Airport as per own arrangements.
  34. End of trip.
  35. ## Summary
  36. 1 night: The Olive Exclusive, Windhoek – Namibia
  37. 3 nights: Kulala Desert Lodge, Sossusvlei – Namibia
  38. 2 nights: Swakopmund Guesthouse, Swakopmund – Namibia
  39. 3 nights: Damaraland Camp, Damaraland – Namibia
  40. 3 nights: Andersson’s at Ongava, Etosha – Namibia
  41. 1 night: The Olive Exclusive, Windhoek – Namibia
