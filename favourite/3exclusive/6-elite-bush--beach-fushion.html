6 Elite Bush & Beach Fushion

3Exclusive

What We Love
  1. Experience the best of bush & beach
  2. Tick off the Big 5 on safari
  3. Stay in luxury – river & ocean views, private pools & butlers
  4. Go off-road driving & search for nocturnal animals
  5. Enjoy water activities, spa treatments & castaway island picnics
  6. Feel like royalty in private helicopter transfers
  7. ## Overview
  8. Day 1:
  9. Upon arrival at Johannesburg International Airport, you will be transferred by light air to Londolozi Varty Camp in the Sabi Sand Reserve, Kruger.
  10. Day 1 – 5:
  11. Spend 4 nights at Londolozi Varty Camp in a luxury chalet on a fully inclusive basis.
  12. Day 5:
  13. Light air transfer from Londolozi Varty Camp to Johannesburg International Airport.
  14. Scheduled flight from Johannesburg to Vilanculos with South African Airways – economy.
  15. Helicopter transfer from Vilanculos International Airport to Azura Benguerra Island in the Bazaruto Archipelago, Mozambique.
  16. Day 5 – 10:
  17. Spend 5 nights at Azura Benguerra Island in an infinity beach villa on a fully inclusive basis.
  18. (Inclusive activities are: a Land Rover island drive, a sunset dhow cruise, use of snorkelling equipment, use of kayaks & paddleboards, various honeymoon & special treats, various kids activities.)
  19. Day 10:
  20. Helicopter transfer from Azura Benguerra Island to Vilanculos International Airport.
  21. Scheduled flight from Vilanculos to Johannesburg with South African Airways – economy.
  22. Depart Johannesburg International Airport as per own arrangements.
  23. End of trip.
  24. ## Summary
  25. 4 nights: Londolozi Varty Camp, Sabi Sand/Kruger – South Africa
  26. 5 nights: Azura Benguerra Island, Bazaruto Archipelago - Mozambique
