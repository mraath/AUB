At Africa Unwind, we custom-design luxury holidays to Africa – so each itinerary is tailored to perfection to suit each client’s individual needs.

However, we thought we’ll share some of our favourite trips with you so that you can get a better idea of what a bespoke Africa Unwind vacation looks like.

Our trips are categorized as follow: Charming, Stylish & Exclusive.

Our Charming Trips offer superb value for money - without compromising on the experience, while our Stylish Trips are ideal for the luxury traveller who prefer stylish interiors & fantastic experiences. The Crème de la Crème at Africa Unwind is our Exclusive Trips that are best suited for the discerning traveller who requires high-end lodges, exclusivity & world-class service.

Before we can create a suggested itinerary for you, we’ll need an estimate budget - the starting place from which we plan all vacations.

Please journey with us as we share some of our favourite trips below. What are you waiting for?

Botswana Bliss

Experience the best of Botswana & Vic Falls • Stay in luxury tents • Enjoy both land & water-based safari activities • See the Okavango Delta from a bird’s eye view • Go on game drives, guided walks, boating & mokoro safaris • Witness the majestic Victoria Falls

Limelight On Africa

Experience a boutique-style African holiday • See iconic Table Mountain & Cape Town’s hot spots • Savour delicious farm-to-table cuisine & South Africa’s best wines • Stay in luxury tents on safari • Enjoy thrilling land & water-based wildlife encounters • Search for nocturnal animals with a spotlight

Swanky Zimbabwe

Experience Zimbabwe’s best regions • Enjoy spectacular views over the Zambezi River • Witness the majestic Vic Falls • Go on game drives, guided walks, canoe trips, boat safaris & fishing expeditions • Experience luxury at its best – private pool, outdoor shower & copper bath • Stay in stylish safari tents

South Africa In Style

Experience the best of South African luxury • Enjoy uninterrupted ocean views & world-class service • Taste fine dining cuisine & award-winning wines • Sleep in style – from an ocean-front retreat to a luxury safari tent • Spend the night in a luxury treehouse • Encounter Africa’s Big 5 from an open 4x4 jeep

Tempting Tanzania

Witness the Great Wildebeest Migration (July – October) • Enjoy excellent Big 5 wildlife viewing year-round • Spot the iconic tree-climbing lions at TarangireGo on open 4x4 day & night drives – searching for nocturnal animals • Enjoy dramatic landscapes & all-day game viewing in the crater • Cycle to a nearby Masai village & meet the locals

Fun For Families

Enjoy views over Cape Town’s iconic Waterfront • Explore Cape Town’s highlights – Table Mountain & penguins at Boulders beach • Stay in a private villa while on safari – dedicated ranger, tracker & butler • See Africa’s famous Big 5 • Experience sea-facing suites & villas on a private beach • Enjoy a wide range of land & water-based island activities (& a Kids Club)
