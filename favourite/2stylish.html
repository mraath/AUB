At Africa Unwind, we custom-design luxury holidays to Africa – so each itinerary is tailored to perfection to suit each client’s individual needs.

However, we thought we’ll share some of our favourite trips with you so that you can get a better idea of what a bespoke Africa Unwind vacation looks like.Our trips are categorized as follow: Charming, Stylish & Exclusive.Our Charming Trips offer superb value for money - without compromising on the experience, while our Stylish Trips are ideal for the luxury traveller who prefer stylish interiors & fantastic experiences. The Crème de la Crème at Africa Unwind is our Exclusive Trips that are best suited for the discerning traveller who requires high-end lodges, exclusivity & world-class service.Before we can create a suggested itinerary for you, we’ll need an estimate budget - the starting place from which we plan all vacations.Please journey with us as we share some of our favourite trips below.
