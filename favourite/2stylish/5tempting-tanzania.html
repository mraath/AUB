Tempting Tanzania

2Stylish

What We Love
  1. Witness the Great Wildebeest Migration (July – October)
  2. Enjoy excellent Big 5 wildlife viewing year-round
  3. Spot the iconic tree-climbing lions at TarangireGo on open 4x4 day & night drives – searching for nocturnal animals
  4. Enjoy dramatic landscapes & all-day game viewing in the crater
  5. Cycle to a nearby Masai village & meet the locals
  6. ## Overview
  7. Day 1:
  8. Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be transferred by road to Legendary Lodge in Arusha, Tanzania.
  9. Overnight at Legendary Lodge in a garden cottage on a bed & breakfast basis.
  10. Day 2:
  11. Road transfer from Legendary Lodge to Arusha Airport.
  12. Light air transfer from Arusha Airport to Sanctuary Swala in Tarangire National Park, Tanzania.
  13. Day 2 – 4:
  14. Spend 2 nights at Sanctuary Swala in a luxury-suite tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  15. Day 4:
  16. Game drive road transfer from Sanctuary Swala through Tarangire National Park & Lake Manyara National Park to Neptune Ngorongoro Luxury Lodge in the Ngorongoro Crater, Tanzania.
  17. Day 4 – 7:
  18. Spend 3 nights at Neptune Ngorongoro Luxury Lodge in a luxury suite on a fully in inclusive basis.
  19. Day 7:
  20. Light air transfer from Neptune Ngorongoro Luxury Lodge to Sanctuary Kichakani (Mobile Camp) in the Serengeti, Tanzania.
  21. Day 7 – 10:
  22. Spend 3 nights at Sanctuary Kichakani in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  23. Day 10:
  24. Light air transfer from Sanctuary Kichakani to Kilimanjaro International Airport.
  25. Depart Kilimanjaro International Airport as per own arrangements.
  26. End of trip.
  27. ## Summary
  28. 1 night: Legendary Lodge, Arusha – Tanzania
  29. 2 nights: Sanctuary Swala Camp, Tarangire – Tanzania
  30. 3 nights: Neptune Ngorongoro Luxury Lodge, Ngorongoro Crater – Tanzania
  31. 3 nights: Sanctuary Kichakani, Serengeti – Tanzania
