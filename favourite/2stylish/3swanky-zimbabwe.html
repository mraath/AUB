What We Love
  1. Experience Zimbabwe’s best regions
  2. Enjoy spectacular views over the Zambezi River
  3. Witness the majestic Vic Falls
  4. Go on game drives, guided walks, canoe trips, boat safaris & fishing expeditions
  5. Experience luxury at its best – private pool, outdoor shower & copper bath
  6. Stay in stylish safari tents
  7. ## Overview
  8. Day 1:
  9. Upon arrival at Victoria Falls International Airport, you will be transferred by road to Matetsi Victoria Falls in Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe.
  10. Day 1 – 4:
  11. Spend 3 nights at Matetsi Victoria Falls in a luxury suite on a fully inclusive basis.
  12. (Activities include game drives, walks, birdwatching, boating, fishing, canoeing & a Guided Tour of Victoria Falls.)
  13. Day 4:
  14. Road transfer from Matetsi Victoria Falls to Victoria Falls International Airport.
  15. Light air transfer from Victoria Falls International Airport to Somalisa Camp in Hwange, Zimbabwe.
  16. Day 4 – 7:
  17. Spend 3 nights at Somalisa Camp in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  18. Day 7:
  19. Light air transfer from Somalisa Camp to Nyamatusi Camp in Mana Pools, Zimbabwe.
  20. Day 7 – 10:
  21. Spend 3 nights at Nyamatusi Camp in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  22. Day 10:
  23. Light air transfer from Nyamatusi Camp to Victoria Falls International Airport.
  24. Depart Victoria Falls International Airport as per own arrangements.
  25. End of trip.
  26. ## Summary
  27. 3 nights: Matetsi Victoria Falls, Victoria Falls – Zimbabwe
  28. 3 nights: Somalisa Camp, Hwange – Zimbabwe
  29. 3 nights: Nyamatusi Camp, Mana Pools – Zimbabwe
