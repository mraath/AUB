3beautiful Botswana

1Charming

What We Love
  1. Enjoy the best wildlife viewing in Africa
  2. See the Okavango Delta from a bird’s eye view (small plane or helicopter)
  3. Experience exciting and & water-based safari activities
  4. Stay at intimate camps in remote settings
  5. Savour spectacular views from the viewing decks
  6. Go on thrilling game drives, guided walks, boating & mokoro safaris
  7. ## Overview
  8. Day 1:
  9. Upon arrival at Maun International Airport, you will be transferred by light air to Camp Moremi in Moremi, Botswana.
  10. Day 1 – 3:
  11. Spend 2 nights at Camp Moremi in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  12. Day 3:
  13. Light air transfer from Camp Moremi to Camp Okavango in the Okavango Delta, Botswana.
  14. Day 3 - 5:
  15. Spend 2 nights at Camp Okavango in a luxury suite on a fully inclusive basis.
  16. Day 5:
  17. Light air transfer from Camp Okavango to Savute Safari Lodge in the Savute, Botswana.
  18. Day 5 - 7:
  19. Spend 2 nights at Savute Safari Lodge in a luxury chalet on a fully inclusive basis.
  20. Day 7:
  21. Light air transfer from Savute Safari Lodge to Maun International Airport.
  22. Depart Maun Airport as per own arrangements.
  23. End of trip.
  24. ## Summary
  25. 2 nights: Camp Moremi, Moremi – Botswana
  26. 2 nights: Camp Okavango, Okavango Delta – Botswana
  27. 2 nights: Savute Safari Lodge, Savute – Botswana
