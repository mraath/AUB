What We Love
  1. Experience Botswana & Vic Falls’ premier lodges
  2. Stay in luxury – spacious suites, private pools & outdoor showers
  3. Encounter Africa’s predators during day & night drives
  4. Enjoy river views & romantic sunset boat cruises
  5. Witness the majestic Victoria Falls
  6. Swim at the edge of the Falls – season dependent
  7. ## Overview
  8. Day 1:
  9. Upon arrival at Maun International Airport, you will be transferred by light air to Vumbura Plains in the Okavango Delta, Botswana.
  10. Day 1 – 4:
  11. Spend 3 nights at Vumbura Plains in a luxury suite on a fully inclusive basis.
  12. Day 4:
  13. Light air transfer from Vumbura Plains to Zarafa Camp in Selinda, Botswana.
  14. Day 4 - 7:
  15. Spend 3 nights at Zarafa Camp in a luxury tent on a fully inclusive basis.
  16. Day 7:
  17. Light air transfer from Zarafa Camp to Livingstone International Airport, Zambia.
  18. Road transfer from Livingstone International Airport to Thorntree River Lodge in Livingstone, Zambia.
  19. Day 7 - 10:
  20. Spend 3 nights at Thorntree River Lodge in a luxury suite on a fully inclusive basis.
  21. (Activities include game drives in Mosi-oa-Tunya National Park, rhino tracking, bush walks, sunrise & sunset boat cruises, Simonga Village visits, a visit to the Zambian side of the Falls, a visit to the Zimbabwean side of the Falls in low water season (exclude visa fees), a visit to the Livingstone Museum, canoe trips, a visit to Maramba & Makuni Markets & catch & release fishing.)
  22. Day 10:
  23. Road transfer from Thorntree River Lodge to Livingstone International Airport.
  24. Depart Livingstone International Airport as per own arrangements.
  25. End of trip.
  26. ## Summary
  27. 3 nights: Vumbura Plains, Okavango Delta – Botswana
  28. 3 nights: Zarafa Camp, Selinda – Botswana
  29. 3 nights: Thorntree River Lodge, Livingstone – Zambia
